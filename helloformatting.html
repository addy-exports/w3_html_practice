Local Lunatic Deforms Text!

Report from Caldwell Sanders, Word Crimes Division

At 5am this morning police were called to an a house in the lower east district over reports of a strange and distorted text. Although authorities were at first skeptical, memories of the Night of Fragmented Fonts ensured that this possible threat was at the very least investigated.

Upon arrival they recieved no answer at the door, however a glimpse of some unsettling paper scraps crumpled up amonst the begonias provided sufficient grounds to search the premise while a propper warrant was rushed through. Once inside, the the building appeared to be abandoned. No sign of the family that was supposed to be living there. Instead the officers found papers. Stacks and stacks of them covered in the most horrificly distorted text imaginable. One person on the scene who wished to remain anonymous claims to have even seen glimpses of Comic Sans.

Needless to say, the building was burned and the surrounding area delclared unfit for human habitation. The city offered the nearby residences buyout offers and all but one were accepted. This comes right on the heels of tensions regarding a budget shortage, which this incident will not doubt be swept up in. Please see page 8B for the continuation of this story.
